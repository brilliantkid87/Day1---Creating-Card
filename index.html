Elon Musk is a renowned entrepreneur, inventor, and industrial designer. He was born in South Africa in 1971 and is now a citizen of the United States and Canada. He is particularly well-known for his work on developing and promoting electric cars through Tesla, and for his efforts to revolutionize space exploration with SpaceX.
