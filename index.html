Changpeng Zhao, also known as CZ, is a Chinese-Canadian entrepreneur and the founder and CEO of Binance, one of the world's largest cryptocurrency exchanges by trading volume. He was born on November 17, 1976, in Jiangsu, China, and grew up in Vancouver, Canada. Before founding Binance in 2017, Zhao worked in various roles in the tech and finance industries, including at Blockchain.info and OKCoin. He is known for his expertise in cryptocurrency trading and his entrepreneurial spirit. Under Zhao's leadership, Binance has grown rapidly and expanded into various areas of the cryptocurrency industry, including launching its own blockchain platform, Binance Smart Chain, and introducing a range of innovative products and services. As of 2021, Binance reportedly has over 10 million registered users and processes billions of dollars in daily trading volume.
